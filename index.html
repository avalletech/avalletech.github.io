About

Background

I'm an IT student and aspiring network/systems professional based in Queens, NY. I'm finishing a BTECH in Computer Systems at CUNY New York City College of Technology (May 2027), building on an AAS in Computer Information Systems I completed in 2025.

My hands-on work spans network simulation in Cisco Packet Tracer, virtualization with Hyper-V, VMware, VirtualBox, and AWS EC2, Active Directory administration, web server configuration with Apache, packet analysis with Wireshark, and scripting in Python and PowerShell.

Before pivoting to IT full-time, I spent nearly five years in telecom at T-Mobile as a Mobile Expert — troubleshooting devices, diagnosing connectivity issues, and resolving escalated technical problems for customers daily. That experience gave me something lab work alone can't: real communication skills under pressure.

I'm bilingual in English and Spanish and actively working toward CompTIA A+ and Network+ to formalize what I've been building in the lab.
